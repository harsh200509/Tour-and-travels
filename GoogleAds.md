**Core technical**

* Mobile-first responsive layout across all screen sizes
* Fast load time (<3s), optimized images, compressed assets
* Stable layout (no CLS shifts), predictable rendering
* Cross-browser compatibility (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, in-app browsers)
* Fallbacks for modern formats (WebP → JPEG/PNG)
* Clean URL structure, no broken links
* HTTPS enabled, no mixed content
* Minimal JS blocking, avoid heavy scripts

**Landing page quality (critical for Google Ads)**

* Message match between ad and landing page
* Clear headline, clear value proposition
* Visible CTA without scrolling
* No misleading claims or bait content
* Real content, not thin/placeholder pages
* Functional navigation, no dead buttons

**Conversion system**

* Fast, simple forms (minimum fields)
* Working checkout / lead capture
* Trust signals (reviews, testimonials, badges)
* Clear pricing and offer transparency
* No forced redirects or pop-up traps

**Compliance / policy**

* Privacy policy page
* Terms of service
* Contact information (email, address if applicable)
* Cookie consent if tracking used
* No restricted or misleading content

**Tracking & data**

* Proper conversion tracking setup
* Analytics installed (events, funnels)
* No duplicate or broken tracking pixels
* Page loads even if tracking scripts fail

**Performance monitoring**

* Track bounce rate, load speed, conversions
* Monitor errors (404, image fail, script fail)
* Test on low-end devices and slow networks

**Security**

* Protect forms from spam (captcha or logic)
* No exposed APIs or keys
* Regular updates of dependencies

**Scalability**

* Handles traffic spikes without crashing
* CDN usage for global delivery
* Backend response time stable under load

**User experience**

* Readable fonts, proper contrast
* No intrusive ads or auto-play media
* Logical flow from entry → action
* Works without login unless necessary

Failure in any one layer propagates into wasted ad spend, lower quality score, or account risk.
